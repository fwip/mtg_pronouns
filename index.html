What's this about?

To investigate the suitability of singular "they" in the card game Magic: The Gathering

Huh?

Well, Magic cards use the awkward "he or she" construct anytime they need to refer to a player. Some players are not accurately described by "he or she," preferring another pronoun or none at all. Also, some cards sound really clunky:

"If a player would draw a card except the first one he or she draws in his or her draw step each turn, that player discards a card instead. If the player discards a card this way, he or she draws a card. If the player doesn't discard a card this way, he or she puts the top card of his or her library into his or her graveyard."
That's six instances of "he or she" or "his or her" in three sentences!

So...

So, I made a simple ruby project that auto-replaces "he or she" with "they" and "his or her" with "their."
